How it works?

Teachers at your school input their course information, philosophy, and methods. iRate presents these reports in an easy-to-use site where students can find their potential teachers, courses, dual enrollment/online options, and leave ratings. This outlet for students and teachers will inspire change due to accountability and assist in students taking a better path for their education journey. iRateâ€™s purpose is to improve teacher-student relationships at Oakland Tech, one evaluation at a time.

User Guidelines

iRate wants to create a safe, productive environment for both students and teachers. This app is an opportunity for teachers to advertise their classes and teachings to students. This course catalog provides the information needed for an independent student, if the options provided by iRate are not sufficient feel free to contact us.

Features

  • Course Catalog

    Course description, teacher, room #, expectations, and alternatives will be accessible. Students can download the syllabus and comprehend what the classes have to offer.

  • Teacher rating

    iRate will provide a safe space for students to leave simple ratings on their teachers. The difficulty, how likely would you recommend, quality, type of grading, and textbook use will be just some of the parameters students can rate. Inappropriate reviews will be dealt with swiftly, as our biggest focus is benefitting student-teacher relationships in the purest way possible. This will lead to the pavement of better educational pathways for all deserving high schoolers in our city of Oakland.
